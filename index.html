Selected Work

NASA Dragonfly Mission (Collins Aerospace)

Contributed to the development of optoelectronic systems for NASA’s Dragonfly mission, including fixture design in SolidWorks and ZEMAX for lens systems, supporting environmental testing and system integration.

Optical Encoder Design

Designed and built a compact optical encoder integrating infrared sensors, QAM, and microcontroller logic to support RTV bond inspection within precision optics systems.

Cold Atom Laser System (Goodsell Lab at Middlebury College)

Developed an experimental setup to laser-cool rubidium atoms and implemented fiber optics control for quantum behavior observations. Funded by a $5000 SRPS research grant.

Spectral Energy Distributions of Quasars (Glikman Lab at Middlebury College)

Analyzed spectral data from 147 quasars using Python, GALEX, and Hubble archives. Created SED graphs and contributed to a publication on red quasar classifications and star formation.
