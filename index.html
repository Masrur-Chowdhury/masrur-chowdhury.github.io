NASA Dragonfly Mission: Thermal cycling and fatigue test of optical components (Testing/Validation)

Designed and built a compact optical encoder integrating IR sensors, QAM signal processing, and microcontroller logic to support RTV bond inspection in precision optics systems. Increased detection speed 4× and reduced reliance on commercial solutions.
