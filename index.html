Selected Work

NASA Dragonfly Mission (Collins Aerospace)

Used SolidWorks and ZEMAX to design tooling and alignment fixtures for optoelectronic assemblies in support of NASAs Dragonfly mission. Enabled repeatable subsystem builds using adhesive bonding and tight-tolerance alignment. Evaluated performance under thermal cycling and vibration testing, contributing to spaceflight environmental qualification.

Optical Encoder Design

Designed and built a compact optical encoder integrating IR sensors, QAM signal processing, and microcontroller logic to support RTV bond inspection in precision optics systems. Increased detection speed 4 and reduced reliance on commercial solutions.

Embedded Systems Validation

Developed a remote test platform using microcontrollers and power analyzers to validate sensing hardware for next-gen aircraft fuel gauging. Led test protocol development across electrical and mechanical failure modes.

Liquid Hydrogen Sensor Testing

Managed cryogenic test workflows for 100+ hydrogen sensors. Created structured datasets to track mechanical failure patterns and improve sensor calibration across extreme conditions.

Cold Atom Laser System (Goodsell Lab, Middlebury College)

Designed and assembled a 3-laser rubidium cooling system with fiber optic routing and thermal management. Integrated precision alignment tools to support quantum behavior observation under SRPS grant funding.

Quasar Classification Research (Glikman Lab)

Analyzed data from 147 quasars using Hubble, GALEX, and WISE. Created SED models and data visualizations to support a publication on red quasar classifications and star formation history.
